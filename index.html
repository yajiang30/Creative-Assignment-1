Cooking

I enjoy cooking for myself everyday. I find it relaxing coming home and cooking dinner. I enjoy planning meals ahead or embrace my creative side by just taking a look at what I have in the fridge and making something on the fly. I like every cuisine and is always looking forward to trying to make new dishes.

Japanese Food

I remember going to Japantown with my parents all the time and grabbing a bento from the supermarket. Japanese food is my favorite because the flavor is simple but delicious. I learned how to make Japanese food mainly from YouTube. Link to a Cooking Channel

Other Cuisines

I enjoy all types of food. Growing up in San Francisco, I got to try all types of cuisines. Mexican, Korean, Greek, Vietnamese, Italian, and many more. Making Chinese food while being away from home reminds me of the home cooked meals I used to eat everyday.
